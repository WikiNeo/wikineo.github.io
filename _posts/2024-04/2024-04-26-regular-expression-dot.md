---
title: "JS Regular Expression /./g"
published: true
tags: RegularExpression
---

```javascript
/./g
```

## Explanation

- `.` matches any character (except for line terminators)
- Global pattern flags 
  - `g` modifier: global. All matches (don't return after first match)
